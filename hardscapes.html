Hardscapes

What landscaping alone cannot accomplish a hardscape can.

What is a hardscape? Picture the Mona Lisa without a frame...simply put, a hardscape frames your vision and draws the eye to centerpieces of the landscape design.

The most common forms of hardscapes are:

  • Fence
  • Decks and Patios
  • Flower-boxes and Built-ins
  • Retaining Walls
  • Waterscapes

Fences are the most common form of hardscape. However, you shouldn't think of a fence as simply a barier. A fence allows you to create a background by blocking out the view of something unappealing, like your alleyway, and also act as a backdrop to a beautiful pond. That's why Aztec offers a variety of cedar, wrought iron, and brick solutions. Have a look at the gallery and see what we can do!

A Patio is an ideal palce to commune with family and guests. Patios are meant to be elegant as well as functional. A wise homeowner knows that a beautifully designed patio can satisfy in a way that no other improvement can. It is a tribute to your taste and a statement of elegance. Have a look at the gallery and see what we can do!

Flower-boxes and Built-ins, while more rare, allow the home to elevate your flora to garner more attention. Flower-boxes give the homeowner an ideal place to display their season botanies and decor. Nothing adds dimension to your habitat like a flower-box. Built-ins, similar to flower-boxes are meant to integrate other conventional items into the environment, such as outdoor kitchens, bars, even refrigerators! Have a look at the gallery and see what we can do!

Retaining Walls serve not only a technical purpose of managing run off, but elevate your landscape. Retaining walls allow the homeonwner to capitalize on the slope of a yard to create a magestic theme in your habitat. Have a look at our gallery and see what we can do!

Waterscapes, like ponds and fountains, are often integrated with retaining walls to add a 4th dimension to the picture, that of sound. Nothing is more relaxing or romantic than hearing the cascading flow of water as part of the ambiance while you pass the time with your loved ones. Have a look at the gallery and see what we can do!
